Triceps

Pushdown

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height.
2. Palms should be facing forward, and elbows should be bent at a 90-degree angle.
3. Push the dumbbells upward until your arms are fully extended, but not locked.
4. Slowly lower the dumbbells back to the starting position.

Cable Crossover Triceps Extension

1. Sit on a bench with back support, holding a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height.
2. Palms should be facing forward, and elbows should be bent at a 90-degree angle.
3. Push the dumbbells upward until your arms are fully extended, but not locked.
4. Slowly lower the dumbbells back to the starting position.

Seated Dumbbell Triceps Extension

1. Set up a cable machine with a rope attachment at a high pulley position.
2. Kneel down on one knee in front of the machine, facing away from it.
3. Hold the rope handles with both hands, arms extended in front of you.
4. Pull the rope towards your chest, keeping elbows pointed out and squeeze your shoulder blades.
5. Pause for a moment at the end of the movement and slowly return to the starting position.
