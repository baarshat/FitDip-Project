Biceps

Barbell Curl

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height.
2. Palms should be facing forward, and elbows should be bent at a 90-degree angle.
3. Push the dumbbells upward until your arms are fully extended, but not locked.
4. Slowly lower the dumbbells back to the starting position.

Dumbbell Curl

1. Sit on a bench with back support, holding a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height.
2. Palms should be facing forward, and elbows should be bent at a 90-degree angle.
3. Push the dumbbells upward until your arms are fully extended, but not locked.
4. Slowly lower the dumbbells back to the starting position.

EZ Bar Curl

1. Set up a cable machine with a rope attachment at a high pulley position.
2. Kneel down on one knee in front of the machine, facing away from it.
3. Hold the rope handles with both hands, arms extended in front of you.
4. Pull the rope towards your chest, keeping elbows pointed out and squeeze your shoulder blades.
5. Pause for a moment at the end of the movement and slowly return to the starting position.

Concentration Curl

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height.
2. Palms should be facing forward, and elbows should be bent at a 90-degree angle.
3. Push the dumbbells upward until your arms are fully extended, but not locked.
4. Slowly lower the dumbbells back to the starting position.

Incline Dumbbell Curls

1. Sit on a bench with back support, holding a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height.
2. Palms should be facing your body, and elbows bent at a 90-degree angle.
3. As you press the dumbbells upward, rotate your palms away from your body.
4. Continue to press until your arms are fully extended, with palms facing forward.
5. Reverse the motion to bring the dumbbells back down, rotating your palms back towards your body.

Preacher Curl

1. Set the lever machine with the handles at shoulder height.
2. Sit with back flat against the pad and grip the handles.
3. Grip the handles with your arms extended and hands at shoulder height.
4. Press the handles forward until your arms are almost fully extended, but not locked.
5. Slowly bring the handles back to the starting position.

Machine Biceps Curl

1. Set the cable machine with the handles at the lowest position.
2. Stand facing opposite the machine, holding the handle arms fully extended.
3. Keep the arms straight and raise them straight in front of you until they are at shoulder height.
4. Slowly lower the handles back down to the starting position.

Cable Curl

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand.
2. Keep the arms straight and raise them straight in front of you until they are at shoulder height.
3. Slowly lower the dumbbells back down to the starting position.

EZ Bar Preacher Curl

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand.
2. Maintain a slight bend in your elbows.
3. Raise both arms out to the sides until they are at shoulder level.
4. Slowly lower the dumbbells back down to your sides.

Dumbbell Preacher Curl

1. Set the cable machine with the handles at the lowest position.
2. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a handle in one hand.
3. Maintain a slight bend in your elbows.
4. Raise the arm out to the sides until it is at shoulder level.
5. Slowly lower the handles back down to your sides.
